# Product Brief for Kabletown Support Assistant

Helpy McHelpface helps Kabletown automate common support interactions without worsening trust or satisfaction.

## Product Thesis

**Reduced user frustration**
The current assistant mishandles intent and escalates too late. Helpy lowers frustration by understanding intent well enough to resolve routine issues or hand off earlier.

**Lowered support toil**
Routine account changes and diagnostics still require too much human effort. Helpy reduces backlog by completing common tasks directly and reserving agents for exceptions.

## Antithesis/Risks

- Helpy will take actions it should not, causing users to be unpleasantly surprised.
- The knowledge base may not provide the context Helpy needs.
- Escalations may still arrive too late, damaging trust.

## Target Audience

**Account support user**
Tinker Tia is an existing customer who wants to change service or hardware without waiting for a representative.

**Technical support user**
Sad Lisa is a customer with a service problem who wants a fast answer or a fast escalation.

## Product Goals

The goals are better support outcomes and lower support toil.

**Adoption metric**
Increase the number of fully automated support interactions from 15% to 65%.

**Value metric**
Improve customer satisfaction ratings after assistant and human support interactions from 2.1 to 3.5 out of 5.

**KPI**
Profit-neutral. Kabletown may reduce human support expense over time, but it may also lower revenue by making downgrades easier. Track downgrades to understand revenue impact.

## North Star Scenarios

**Account change**
Tinker Tia wants to remove an unused cable box and lower her bill. Helpy finds the matching plan change, explains the savings, creates the return flow, and follows up when the box is received. The interaction ends with a survey and a completed downgrade without agent involvement.

**Outage escalation**
Sad Lisa loses internet service and asks Helpy what happened. Helpy checks the outage map, explains there is an outage, shares the ETA, and follows up when service returns. If the outage data is unclear or she is angry, Helpy hands the conversation to a representative with the context attached.

## Why This Example Works

- The thesis is about outcomes, not features.
- The risks could invalidate the thesis.
- The metrics show adoption, user value, and business impact.
- The scenarios cover both self-serve success and escalation.
